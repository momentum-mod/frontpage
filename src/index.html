What is Momentum Mod?

Momentum Mod is a free, standalone game built on the Strata Source Engine that aims to faithfully recreate, centralize, and improve upon the movement game modes found in Counter-Strike, Team Fortress, Half-Life 2, Titanfall 2, and Quake.

Features

Leaderboards, replays and analytics

All runs submitted are placed on a global leaderboard, including a replay that can be watched by anyone.

Our game UI and website provide detailed analytics for every run, allowing you to track your improvement and compare yourself with other players.

In-game map selector

Maps can be downloaded and played instantly in-game, with advanced filtering and HD screenshots to help you find new ones easily. No more players !rtv-ing you off the map you wanted to play, it's always up to you.

Maps are submitted and reviewed by the community, including ports of existing maps from your favorite gamemodes!

Level up!

Momentum is heavily geared towards player progression and rewards you for all runs you submit.

Submitting runs earn you experience points, which are public on your profile, as well as rank points for that gamemode, which contribute to your overall gamemode rank.

0 ping multiplayer

Our multiplayer uses Steam lobbies to establish direct connections between users.

Whilst players aren't kept perfectly in sync with one-another (so no combat surf/trikz/etc...), it performs well for spectating and observing players on the map.

The upside is that anyone can host a lobby, completely for free, and have no movement delay unless they're spectating another player, and can be in a lobby with someone whilst on different maps.
